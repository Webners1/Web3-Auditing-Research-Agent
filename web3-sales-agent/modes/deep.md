# Mode: deep - Pre-Call Research Brief

Use this after stage 2 is complete and preferably after stage-3 `evaluate`.

This mode adds conversation prep on top of the stage-2 report and stage-3 sales brief. It does not replace either one.

---

## Required Inputs

Read:
1. `data/research-handoffs/{slug}.md`
2. the linked stage-2 report from `../audit-output/`
3. the stage-3 sales brief from `reports/` if it exists

If the handoff is missing, stop and send the workflow back to stage 2.

---

## Brief Format

```markdown
## Deep Research: [Protocol] - Pre-Pitch Brief

**Date:** {TODAY}
**Primary source:** stage-2 report + fresh public checks

### 1. What the Protocol Actually Does
- core primitive
- user base
- business model

### 2. Team And Visibility
- visible contributors
- public team presence
- doxxed vs anonymous

### 3. Recent Moves
- recent commits
- recent announcements
- governance or launch activity

### 4. Publicly Visible Problem
- the pain they already appear aware of
- where users or the team are feeling it

### 5. Upcoming Milestone
- launch, migration, audit window, governance vote, major release

### 6. Competitive Context
- relevant competitors
- where this protocol is stronger or weaker

### 7. My Angle
- strongest proof point
- best first question
- likely objection and response
```

---

## Notes

- verify rapidly changing facts before a call
- favor sources tied to the stage-2 report and official project channels
- keep the brief pitch-oriented, not generic research for research's sake
